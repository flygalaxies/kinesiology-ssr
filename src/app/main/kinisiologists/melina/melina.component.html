ABOUT ME

Melina has always had a passion for assisting in the physical and emotional healing of mankind. The wellbeing of the human body and spirit is of paramount importance to Melina. Her journey with kinesiology, brought about the realisation that the human body was created in such perfection, allowing it to heal within itself. She recognises the importance of teaching and empowering people, to actually take responsibility for their own health and wellbeing. The slightest change and improvement is experienced as a massive shift and victory. Melina equally loves interacting with humans as well as with animals and nature. She enjoys camping and the outdoors and is an avid sports follower. “If you are serious about your own health and wellbeing, and you are prepared to take personal responsibility for your own wellbeing, I would love to help and guide you on this wonderful journey. It will not only change your health, it ll change your life, at home, in the workplace and, on the sports fields.”
